Privacy Policy for Toji Workout App

Last Updated: October 23, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Toji Workout App is a personal fitness tracking application. We collect and store the following information locally in your browser:

  • Workout data (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
  • Body measurements and progress photos
  • Nutrition information (macros, calories)
  • Fitness goals and achievements

2. Google Fit Integration

When you connect Google Fit, we access:

  • Activity data (steps, calories burned)
  • Heart rate information
  • Body measurements

This data is used solely to display your fitness metrics within the app and is not shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

All your personal data is stored:

  • Locally: In your browser's local storage
  • Cloud (Optional): In your personal Firebase account if you enable cloud sync

We do not have access to your data. You maintain full control and ownership.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Access your data on our servers

5. Google API Services

Toji Workout App's use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all your data at any time by clearing browser storage
  • Revoke Google Fit access at Google Account Permissions
  • Export your data from the app settings

7. Security

Your data is protected by:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • OAuth 2.0 for secure Google Fit authentication
  • Local browser storage security

8. Contact

For privacy concerns or questions, contact: ayodejisadiku@gmail.com

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
